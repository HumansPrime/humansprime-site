Putting Humans Firmly in Charge

In an increasingly automated world, Humans Prime™ PBC was founded to ensure human safety and maintain public trust in autonomous technology.

A New Standard in Robotic Safety

Humans Prime™ is a Public Benefit Corporation committed to human oversight in robotics. Our founding team brings decades of real-world experience from designing and manufacturing master switches and critical components for high-stakes robotics since 2001. This deep expertise in fail-safe systems uniquely positions us to address the safety challenges of tomorrow.

Introducing Our First Product

The ARK Switch™ (Patent Pending)

The Autonomous Robot Kill Switch (ARK Switch™) is a groundbreaking, standalone safety module designed to empower any human with immediate, decisive control over any AI-powered machine.

It provides a universal, independent override that physically cuts main power in a single action, operating entirely separate from the robot's internal software. The ARK Switch™ is a direct response to the critical need for a human-centric failsafe in the age of automation.

Why It's Essential

Human Control First

It reasserts human primacy in environments shared with AI-powered machines, ensuring safety remains paramount.

A Universal Procedure

It provides one common, intuitive interface, eliminating the need to learn complex shutdown procedures for different machines.

Boosts Public Trust

A visible, independent safeguard fosters greater public confidence in the deployment of autonomous technologies.
