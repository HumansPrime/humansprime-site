About Humans Prime™

Our Mission

The protection and advancement of human welfare by researching, developing, and distributing technologies and standards that ensure autonomous robots and artificial-intelligence systems operate under human control and prioritize human safety, dignity, and well-being.

Decades of Robotics Expertise

Humans Prime™ PBC, founded in 2025, brings together a team with unparalleled, real-world experience in robotics safety and control systems. The founders have been at the forefront of designing and manufacturing master switches and other critical components for lethal fighting robots since 2001 through their Team Whyachi Robotics brand.

This extensive background in high-stakes, high-performance robotics has instilled a deep understanding of fail-safe systems and the absolute necessity of robust human control. Furthermore, the founding team has a long-standing history of collaboration with leading humanoid robotics companies, manufacturing precision parts for over a decade. This intimate knowledge of advanced autonomous systems, combined with a combat robotics heritage, uniquely positions Humans Prime™ PBC to address the evolving safety challenges in the rapidly expanding world of AI-powered machines.
